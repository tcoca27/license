Wide Open Shot Recommender

We created a system which helps in finding the most open player during an attacking sequence.

To analyze a play you should create an account and add a video of an attacking sequence. The starting point of the video should be when the attacking team reaches the front court. After analysis, you will be able to see the results on the results page.
